Technologies Used

These are some of the technologies I have used so far.

Cal Emulator

My partner and I made a character for character emulation of the native Cal app.

Tim Creasy and I made this app at Nashville Software School as a NodeJS console app. I am really proud of it becasue I feel like we used a modular and unique approach to mathematically remake the Cal app. We also used Mocha testing to make sure that ever character, even whitespaces, were identical to the native Cal app.

Checkout our GitHub repo for this project.

Bar Wars

A fighting app based on the age old conflict of bar patrons vs. staff.

I was assigned as the team lead for this project where we were tasked with making a fun fighting app. Over the three days that we worked on it I helped us plan a workflow, stick to it, and execute a great project. Thanks for good focus and time management we made a fun project.

Here is our GitHub repo for this project! Check out my teammate GitHubs too.
